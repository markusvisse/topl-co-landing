  Empowering Growth By Enabling Investment


  • Begin

Topl bridges the boundaries that keep capital locked up

The idea behind Topl is simple: build the financial infrastructure necessary to bring low-cost capital to developing economies. The barriers that hinder investment into developing economies not only deprive many regions of the capital necessary for an economy to prosper but also block investors from accessing premier opportunities. To break down these barriers and enable low-friction investments anywhere in the world, Topl is developing a public blockchain to source, execute, and track these investments and their outcomes.

Features

Topl is committed to providing a secure, accessible, and powerful method to invest anywhere in the world. Guided by this aim, we’ve focused on developing these key features.

Smart Investment Structure

Topl’s investment platform connects companies and investors through joint ventures, better aligning the interests of companies and investors. Then to reduce friction and maximize returns, contracts can be settled in Polys or with tokenized or physical assets.

Integrated Reputation Scoring

All participants earn a score based on contract outcomes. This score, based past behavior and the one's network, allows Investors to avoid fraud, helps effective Hubs gain legitimacy, and gives competitive Producers leverage for improved terms.

Fundamentally Stable Currency

Polys are backed by a basket of commodity, utility, and currency assets, ensuring value-stability. Because the Poly is backed by the production on the Topl platform itself, there’s no need to rely on one nation’s monetary policy, making it ideal for cross-border investment.

Interoperable Tokens

Polys and Arbits, the Topl staking token, will both be available as ERC20 tokens on Ethereum and other blockchains. The ability to move tokens between these chains and Topl will be handled through off-chain cryptographic transactions, enabling liquid markets and robust access.

Granular Privacy Controls

To accommodate the complex needs of real-world markets, we enable anonymous investment agreements to coexist with the need for public financial disclosures by combining an encrypted public bulletin and non-linkable transactions.

Prediction Markets

History alone can’t provide the complete risk profile of an investment. So Topl employs prediction markets to incorporate additional information into our reputation scores, rewarding experts and local partners for their contributions to the system.

Process

Topl is more than just a cryptocurrency, an exchange, or a database of reputable investment opportunities. It's a complete investment platform, a single solution that enables investments to be vetted, deals negotiated, contracts executed, and funds transferred. While every investment on the Topl platform is unique, here's a simple example to get started.

  • Begin

Request for Investment

A Producer seeking capital may use Topl’s blockchain platform to find an investment partner for a joint venture. The Producer publishes the requested amount along with proposed terms, including what's to be produced, project location, and profit share, through the peer-to-peer blockchain network to be viewed by potential Investors.

  • Continue →

Contract Negotiation

After an investment proposal has been submitted, potential Investors may view the proposal and summary information regarding the Producer. If an Investor believes they may be interested in financing the project, they submit their signature to the Producer, indicating interest. The Producer can then decide to accept or reject the overture and negotiations may begin.

  • Continue →

Signing & Submission

Once the Producer and Investor have reached an agreement for their joint venture, only one additional step is required. To facilitate the disbursement of funds and handle the sale of the assets produced, a third entity known as a Hub serves as an intermediary. A Hub, most often chosen for its proximity to the Producer, acts as an essential link between financial transactions on the blockchain and the movement of real goods.

  • Continue →

Fund Transfer & Disbursement

On the Topl platform, transfers and balances are denominated either in Polys, the platform’s native cryptocurrency, or in asset tokens, representing physical commodities or currencies. A signed and submitted contract proposal enters into force only with the transfer of the appropriate number of asset tokens from the Investor into escrow administered by the Hub. Once funded, the Hub dispenses the tokens to the Producer.

  • Continue →

Production Delivery

After receiving the funds, the Producer begins work on satisfying the contract. Along with a schedule for fulfillment, the contract specifies either one-time or periodic delivery of production to the Hub. The use of the Hub in this function serves a dual purpose: to provide the Producer with a streamlined mechanism to offload commodity assets and to allow the Investor to remain confident in the fulfillment of the contract.

  • Continue →

Contract Fulfillment & Payment

After the Producer completes delivery, which must be confirmed by the Hub, the contract is considered fulfilled. At this time, the Hub dispenses asset tokens to the Producer and Investor based on the profit share dictated in the contract. Finally, the state of the blockchain reflects this fulfilled and paid contract, and the reputation scores of all involved parties are updated to reflect the outcome of the contract.

Roadmap

This is intended to be a forward-looking perspective on the expected milestones for development of the Topl network. These are subject to minor changes as implementation moves forward.

Whitepaper Published

The first draft of the whitepaper, discussing the economic details and technical overview of the project, is made publicly viewable.

June 2017

Alpha Client Release

First testnet client is released for early evaluation of networking, security, and functionality.

July 2017

Public Testnet Launch

Updated testnet client made available, and bootstrapping nodes will always be up. Transfer, initial consensus protocol, and basic investment flow can be evaluated.

July 2017

Initial Yellowpaper Release

First draft of the yellowpaper is released detailing technical specifications of the network — both current and future.

July 2017

Arbit Sale Announcement

Information regarding the future sale of Arbits — such as schedule, preliminary terms, and process — is disseminated.

August 2017

Bug Bounty & Tester Rewards Begin

Network testers and those who discover network vulnerabilities or flaws will begin being rewarded with Arbits that will be distributed in the genesis block.

September 2017

Feature Freeze for Mainnet Launch

Development of additional functionality halts. Focus shifts to further security testing, code review, and client platform support.

November 2017

First Platform Investments Secured

First round of prospective businesses and capital are committed to a large-scale trial of the system.

February 2018

Mainnet Launch

The mainnet is launched for a trial investments to begin. Arbits are distributed according to initial sale results and distribution allotment.

March 2018

Fulfillment of Initial Platform Investments

The initial set of investments made on the Topl platform begins to see fulfillment.

November 2018

First Independent Hub Operators

Hubs not directly associated with Topl begin servicing investment contracts through the platform.
